## Who is Unii?

**Unii** is a professional [Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang)
player who competes as a **[jungler](/wiki/glossary/jungler)** for
[Selangor Red Giants](/wiki/teams/selangor-red-giants) in
[MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia).

## Season 17

Unii joined SRG in **March 2026** and was part of the roster that won **MPL
Malaysia Season 17** in June 2026 — the organisation's record fifth consecutive
title.
