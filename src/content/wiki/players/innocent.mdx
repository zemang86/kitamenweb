## Who is Innocent?

**Innocent** is a professional [Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang)
player who competes as the **[gold laner](/wiki/glossary/gold-laner)** for
[Selangor Red Giants](/wiki/teams/selangor-red-giants) in
[MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia).

## Season 17 champion

Innocent was part of the SRG roster that won **MPL Malaysia Season 17** in June
2026, the organisation's record fifth consecutive title. As gold laner he is the
team's primary marksman and late-game damage dealer.
