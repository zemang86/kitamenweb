## Who is Sepat?

**Sepat** (real name **Muhammad Irfan bin Aujang**) is a Malaysian professional
**[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang)**
**[EXP laner](/wiki/glossary/exp-laner)** (formerly a gold laner), who came up through
**[HomeBois](/wiki/teams/homebois)**.

He won **[MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia) Season 12 (2023)** with HomeBois and
was runner-up in Seasons 13 and 14, also taking the **Kohai SEA Championship Season 1 (2023)**.
He now plays for **[Team Vamos](/wiki/teams/team-vamos)**.
