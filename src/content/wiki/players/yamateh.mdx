## Who is YamateH?

**YamateH** (real name **Ng Wei Poong**) is a Malaysian **[Dota 2](/wiki/games/dota-2)** legend
— a "first-generation" star of the Malaysian scene, nicknamed "Y-God" since the DotA 1 days.

He represented **[Orange Esports](/wiki/teams/orange-esports)** at
**[The International](/wiki/tournaments/the-international) 2012**, later played for Titan at
TI2014, and won the **2013 Asian Cyber Games** — part of the wave of Malaysian players who put
SEA Dota on the map. He is now inactive as a competitor.
