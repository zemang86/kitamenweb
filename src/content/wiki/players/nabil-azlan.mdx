## Who is Nabil Azlan?

**Nabil Azlan** is a Malaysian racing driver and, with his brother
**[Naquib](/wiki/players/naquib-azlan)**, one of Malaysia's standout
**[sim-to-real](/wiki/glossary/sim-racing)** success stories. He found success on
**[iRacing](/wiki/games/iracing)**, winning the **Asia Pacific Esports Formula Cup** and
representing **Malaysia in Esports F4 at the FIA Motorsport Games** in Valencia.

Mentored at **[Alex Yoong's](/wiki/players/alex-yoong)** academy, he carried that form into
real racing through Toyota Gazoo Racing, winning the **Sepang 1000km (2023)** — the year after
his brother took the same race.

## Frequently asked questions

### What did Nabil Azlan achieve in sim racing?

He won the Asia Pacific Esports Formula Cup and raced for Malaysia in Esports F4 at the FIA
Motorsport Games.

### Did Nabil Azlan move to real racing?

Yes — like his brother Naquib, he progressed from iRacing into real motorsport and won the
Sepang 1000km (2023).
