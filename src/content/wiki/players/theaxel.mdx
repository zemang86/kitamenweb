## Who is TheAxel?

**TheAxel** (real name **Mohamad Azim Bin Mohd Asri**) is a Malaysian professional
**[Garena Free Fire](/wiki/games/free-fire)** player and one of the most decorated names of
the domestic scene.

He won **FFWS Malaysia 2024 Spring**, the back-to-back MCPS Majors S5 and S6 (2023), and
multiple **[FFMC](/wiki/tournaments/free-fire-malaysia-championship)** titles across 2021,
2025 and 2026, and competed at the S-tier FFWS 2022 in Bangkok. His titles are at the **national
(Malaysian) level** — he has not won a Free Fire *World* championship. He plays for Aurora
Gaming.
