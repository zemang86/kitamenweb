## Who is Taj Izrin Aiman?

**Taj Izrin Aiman** is a Malaysian **[sim racer](/wiki/glossary/sim-racing)** who won the
**[Toyota GR GT Cup](/wiki/tournaments/toyota-gr-gt-cup-malaysia)** Asia 2024 **Individual
Champion** title (65 points) on **[Gran Turismo](/wiki/games/gran-turismo)**, then represented
**Asia at the GR GT Cup Global Finals** in December 2024.

His run is one of the strongest recent results by a Malaysian on the Gran Turismo competitive
ladder, showing the country can produce drivers competitive at the regional and global level.

### What did Taj Izrin Aiman win?

The Toyota GR GT Cup Asia 2024 individual title, advancing to represent Asia at the Global

Gran Turismo (GT7), through the Toyota GR GT Cup competition.
