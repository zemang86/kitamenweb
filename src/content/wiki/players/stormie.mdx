## Who is Stormie?

**Stormie** is a professional [Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang)
player who competes as the **[mid laner](/wiki/glossary/mid-laner)** for
[Selangor Red Giants](/wiki/teams/selangor-red-giants) in
[MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia).

## Season 17 champion

Stormie was part of the SRG roster that won **MPL Malaysia Season 17** in June
2026, the organisation's record fifth consecutive title. As mid laner he provides
burst damage and map control from the centre of the map.
