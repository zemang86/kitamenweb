## Who is ChuaN?

**ChuaN** (real name **Wong Hock Chuan**) is a Malaysian **[Dota 2](/wiki/games/dota-2)**
legend — the **first Southeast Asian player to win
[The International](/wiki/tournaments/the-international)**, lifting the Aegis with **Invictus
Gaming** in **2012**. (This Invictus Gaming is the Chinese Dota 2 club, unrelated to the
Malaysian MLBB franchise of the same brand.)

He was also a **runner-up at TI2014 and TI2015**, cementing one of the most decorated careers
in SEA Dota history, before moving into coaching in later years.
