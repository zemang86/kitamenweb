## Who is Ws?

**Ws** (real name **Chung Wei Shen**) is a Malaysian professional
**[Dota 2](/wiki/games/dota-2)** offlaner, best known for a matchmaking milestone: he became
the **first player ever to reach 15,000 MMR** (August 2024), and later pushed past 16,000.

On the competitive side he won the **2022 Commonwealth Esports Championships** representing
Malaysia and has reached tier-1 finals such as DreamLeague Season 29 and PGL Wallachia Season 8.
He plays for **Aurora Gaming**.
