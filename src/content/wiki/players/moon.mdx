## Who is Moon?

**Moon** (real name **Zikry Bin Shamsuddin**) is a retired Malaysian professional
**[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang)**
**[mid laner](/wiki/glossary/mid-laner)**, best known as a long-serving core of **Todak**
(2019–2025) — one of the defining players of Malaysia's early MLBB era.

He is a **three-time [MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia) champion** — winning
MPL MY/SG Season 6 (2020), MPL MY Season 9 (2022) and MPL MY Season 11 (2023) — and took
**3rd at the M1 World Championship (2019)**. He is one of only a handful of players to appear
across the first four
**[M World Championships](/wiki/tournaments/m-series-world-championship)** (M1–M4).
