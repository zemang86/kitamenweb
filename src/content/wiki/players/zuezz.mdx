## Who is Zuezz?

**Zuezz** (real name **Adam Hazwan**) is a Malaysian professional
**[Garena Free Fire](/wiki/games/free-fire)** rusher for Aurora Gaming.

He has won **FFWS Malaysia 2024 Spring**, the MCPS Majors S5 and S6 (2023), and
**[FFMC](/wiki/tournaments/free-fire-malaysia-championship)** 2025 Fall and 2026 Spring, among
other domestic results — a consistent fixture of Malaysia's top Free Fire rosters alongside
**[TheAxel](/wiki/players/theaxel)**.
