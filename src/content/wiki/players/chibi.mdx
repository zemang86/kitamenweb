## Who is Chibi?

**Chibi** (real name **Muhammad Nazhan Mohd Nor**) is a Malaysian professional
**[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang)**
**[jungler](/wiki/glossary/jungler)**, who made his name during a long tenure with
**[HomeBois](/wiki/teams/homebois)** (2020–2024).

He won **[MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia) Season 12 (2023)** with HomeBois and
was runner-up in Seasons 13 and 14, reaching the M5 and M6 World Championships along the way.
In 2025 he was named **MPL Malaysia Season 16 Regular Season MVP and Most Improved Player**.
