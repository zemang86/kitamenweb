## Who is ChYuaN?

**ChYuaN** (real name **Ng Kee Chyuan**) is a Malaysian professional
**[Dota 2](/wiki/games/dota-2)** midlaner and captain.

He reached **[The International](/wiki/tournaments/the-international) 2021** with **Fnatic**,
and earlier won the **StarLadder ImbaTV Minor Season 3** (Team Aster) and a **BTS Pro Series
Season 4 SEA** title (TNC) — a well-travelled career across the SEA and international circuit.
