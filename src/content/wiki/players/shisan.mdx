## Who is Shisan?

**Shisan** (real name **Chong Khean Loong**) is a Malaysian professional
**[Honor of Kings](/wiki/games/honor-of-kings)** mid laner for
**[Geekay Esports](/wiki/teams/geekay-esports)**.

He took **3rd at the Honor of Kings International Championship 2025** (with Alpha Gaming) and
3rd at the Honor of Kings Championship 2024 (Team Secret), and was **runner-up in
[MKL](/wiki/tournaments/mkl) Spring 2026** — one of the steadiest Malaysian performers on the
international stage.
