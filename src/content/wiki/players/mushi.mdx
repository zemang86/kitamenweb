## Who is Mushi?

**Mushi** (real name **Chai Yee Fung**) is widely regarded as **Malaysia's most famous
[Dota 2](/wiki/games/dota-2) player**. A carry and mid-lane star, he led
**[Orange eSports](/wiki/teams/orange-esports)** to a **3rd-place finish at
[The International 2013](/wiki/tournaments/the-international)** — then the best-ever TI
result for a Southeast Asian team.

He went on to anchor the **Team Malaysia → Fnatic** Malaysian-core era of the mid-2010s,
becoming the face of Malaysian Dota 2 for a generation and one of the region's most decorated
players.
