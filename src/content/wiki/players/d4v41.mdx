## Who is d4v41?

**d4v41** (real name **Khalish Rusyaidee**) is a Malaysian professional
**[VALORANT](/wiki/games/valorant)** player from **Kelantan**. He is the
**[in-game leader](/wiki/glossary/igl)** of **Paper Rex (PRX)**, a leading side in
**[VCT Pacific](/wiki/tournaments/vct-pacific)**.

A former CS:GO player, d4v41 became the **first Malaysian to compete at a VCT
international event** and went on to lead Paper Rex to a **runner-up finish at
VALORANT Champions 2023** — the deepest international run by a team with a
Malaysian core. He remains the most accomplished Malaysian in tier-1 VALORANT.
