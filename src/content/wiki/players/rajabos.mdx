## Who is Rajabos?

**Rajabos** is a Malaysian **[EA SPORTS FC](/wiki/games/ea-sports-fc)** (formerly FIFA)
player and the standout local name on EA's **FC Pro** circuit. He won the **FC Pro 24 Open
Regional Cup: Asia South** — EA's regional bracket covering Malaysia and Southeast Asia —
and has appeared at FIFA Global Series playoffs.

He competes on console in EA's **"Asia South"** region. No Malaysian has yet reached the
**[FC Pro World Championship](/wiki/tournaments/fc-pro-world-championship)**, which makes
Rajabos's regional title Malaysia's strongest EA FC result to date. (His real name is not
listed on public competitive records.)
