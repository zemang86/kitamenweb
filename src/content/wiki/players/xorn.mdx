## Who is Xorn?

**Xorn** (real name **Mohammad Zul Hisham Bin Mohd Noor**) is a Malaysian professional
**[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang)**
**[roamer](/wiki/glossary/roamer)** and team captain, known for his shot-calling across a
long MPL career.

He won **[MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia) Season 3 (2019)** with Geek Fam and
**MPL Malaysia Season 12 (2023)** with **[HomeBois](/wiki/teams/homebois)**, where he was named
**Finals MVP**; he was also the **MPL MY/SG Season 5 League MVP (2020)**. He later captained
**[Team Vamos](/wiki/teams/team-vamos)** (2024–2026) and reached the **M6 World Championship**
(7th–8th, 2024).
