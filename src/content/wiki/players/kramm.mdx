## Who is Kramm?

**Kramm** is a professional [Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang)
player who competes as the **[EXP laner](/wiki/glossary/exp-laner)** for
[Selangor Red Giants](/wiki/teams/selangor-red-giants) in
[MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia).

## Season 17 champion

Kramm was part of the SRG roster that won **MPL Malaysia Season 17** in June 2026
— the organisation's record fifth consecutive title. As EXP laner he holds the
side lane and builds the team's frontline in fights.
