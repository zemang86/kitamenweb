## Who is kaze?

**kaze** (real name **Andrew Khong Weng Keong**) is Malaysia's most prominent
**[Counter-Strike 2](/wiki/games/counter-strike-2)** export. An **[AWPer](/wiki/glossary/awp)**,
he built his career almost entirely with **China-based organisations** — **TyLoo**, **Vici
Gaming**, **Flash Gaming** and most recently **Rare Atom**, where he transitioned to an
assistant-coach role in 2025.

Because Malaysia's domestic CS scene is small, kaze reached the international stage by joining
the Chinese circuit rather than through a Malaysian org — a path that made him the country's
best-known Counter-Strike player.
