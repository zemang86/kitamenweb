## Who is MidOne?

**MidOne** (real name **Yeik Nai Zheng**) is a Malaysian **[Dota 2](/wiki/games/dota-2)**
star known as a fearless mid and offlane player. He was the **first Southeast Asian to
reach 8000 MMR**, and reached **4th place at [The International 2019](/wiki/tournaments/the-international)**
with Team Secret.

Across a long career — Fnatic, OG, Team Secret, SMG, Tundra and MOUZ — he remained one of
Malaysia's premier Dota 2 exports, winning **PGL Wallachia Season 6** with MOUZ in late
2025.
