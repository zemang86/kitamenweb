## Who is Yums?

**Yums** is a professional [Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang)
player who competes as the **[roamer](/wiki/glossary/roamer)** for
[Selangor Red Giants](/wiki/teams/selangor-red-giants) in
[MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia).

## Season 17 MVP

Yums was named **MVP of MPL Malaysia Season 17** — taking the award for both the
regular season and the grand final — as SRG won their record fifth consecutive
title in June 2026. As the team's roamer, he anchors the vision, engages, and
pick setups that define SRG's playstyle.
