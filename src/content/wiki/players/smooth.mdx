## Who is Smooth?

**Smooth** (real name **Aeliff Adam Md Ariff**) is a Malaysian professional
**[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang)**
**[EXP laner](/wiki/glossary/exp-laner)**.

He won **[MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia) Season 8 (2021)** with Team SMG, and
in Season 11 (2023) was the **Regular Season MVP** and a finalist. He was also runner-up in
Seasons 12 and 14, with M3, M5 and M6 World Championship appearances, and won the Jaguh All
Star Hotlink event in 2024.
