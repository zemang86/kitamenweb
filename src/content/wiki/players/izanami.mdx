## Who is Izanami?

**Izanami** (real name **Mohammad Irwandy Lim**) is a Malaysian professional
**[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang)**
**[mid laner](/wiki/glossary/mid-laner)**.

He won **[MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia) Season 7 (2021)** with RSG Malaysia
and placed 4th at MSC 2021. After a spell with **[HomeBois](/wiki/teams/homebois)**, he was
runner-up in MPL Malaysia Season 15 (2025) and named Most Improved Player. He later joined
**[AC Esports](/wiki/teams/ac-esports)**.
