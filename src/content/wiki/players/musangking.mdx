## Who is MusangKing?

**MusangKing** (real name **Chan Tat Yann**) is a Malaysian professional
**[Honor of Kings](/wiki/games/honor-of-kings)** jungler — the most decorated Malaysian player
in the game.

He won the **Honor of Kings Championship 2024** (an S-Tier event) with Black Shrew Esport and
was named **Finals MVP**, was on the Team of the Tournament at the 2024 Midseason Invitational,
and won **[MKL](/wiki/tournaments/mkl) Fall 2025**. He now plays for
**[Geekay Esports](/wiki/teams/geekay-esports)**, which fields the former Black Shrew core.
