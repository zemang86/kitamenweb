## Who is Naquib Azlan?

**Naquib Azlan** is a Malaysian racing driver and one of the clearest examples of the
country's **[sim-to-real](/wiki/glossary/sim-racing)** pipeline. He built his skills on
**[iRacing](/wiki/games/iracing)**, was spotted through **[Alex Yoong's](/wiki/players/alex-yoong)**
eRacing events, and progressed into real motorsport via **Toyota Gazoo Racing's Rookie Driver
Development Programme**.

On track he became the **first-ever Rookie-class champion** of Toyota's **Vios Challenge** and
won the **Sepang 1000km (2022)** — a concrete bridge between Malaysia's sim racing scene and
its real motorsport heritage. He races alongside his brother
**[Nabil](/wiki/players/nabil-azlan)**.

## Frequently asked questions

### How did Naquib Azlan start racing?

On the simulator — through iRacing and Alex Yoong's eRacing programme — before graduating to
real-world racing with Toyota Gazoo Racing.

### What has Naquib Azlan won?

The Sepang 1000km (2022) and Toyota's first Vios Challenge Rookie-class title.
