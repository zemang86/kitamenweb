## Who is NothingToSay?

**NothingToSay** (real name **Cheng Jin Xiang**) is a Malaysian professional
**[Dota 2](/wiki/games/dota-2)** midlaner, widely regarded as one of the most accomplished
active Malaysian players in the game.

He was **runner-up at [The International](/wiki/tournaments/the-international) 2021** (his first
TI), won the **Riyadh Masters 2022** and **WePlay AniMajor 2021**, and took 3rd at TI2023. In
early 2026 he reached the **17,000 MMR** milestone. He currently plays for **Xtreme Gaming**.
