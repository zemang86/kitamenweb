## Who is Soloz?

**Soloz** (real name **Mohd Faris bin Zakaria**) is a Malaysian
**[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang)** veteran, content creator
and **co-founder of [Team Vamos](/wiki/teams/team-vamos)** — one of the most recognisable faces
of the Malaysian MLBB scene.

As a player he was the **[MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia) League MVP** twice
(MPL MY/SG Season 2 in 2018 and Season 4 in 2019), won the Season 10 Legend Award (2022), and
took **3rd in Season 11 (2023)** with **[HomeBois](/wiki/teams/homebois)**. Off the rift he is
Malaysia's most-watched MLBB creator, with a following in the millions, and helped build Team
Vamos into a multi-title Malaysian organisation.
