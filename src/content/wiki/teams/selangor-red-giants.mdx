## Who are the Selangor Red Giants?

**Selangor Red Giants (SRG)** are a Malaysian esports organisation and the most
decorated [Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang)
franchise in [MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia) history. They are the
benchmark team of the local MLBB era and a regular Malaysian representative at
international events.

## The record five-peat

On **7 June 2026**, SRG defeated **Team Vamos 4–0** in the best-of-seven grand
final at Stadium Juara, Bukit Kiara, to win **MPL Malaysia Season 17** — their
**fifth consecutive** league title and fifth Immortal Cup, the most of any
organisation in the league's history. Roamer **[Yums](/wiki/players/yums)** was
named MVP for both the regular season and the finals.

The Season 17 final drew a record **25.8 million hours watched** with a peak
audience around **740,000**, making it one of the most-watched MPL Malaysia
broadcasts ever.

## Season 17 roster

The championship lineup blended Malaysian and Filipino talent:

- **[Yums](/wiki/players/yums)** — [roamer](/wiki/glossary/roamer) (MY), S17 MVP
- **[Innocent](/wiki/players/innocent)** — [gold laner](/wiki/glossary/gold-laner) (PH)
- **[Stormie](/wiki/players/stormie)** — [mid laner](/wiki/glossary/mid-laner) (MY)
- **[Sekys](/wiki/players/sekys)** / **[Unii](/wiki/players/unii)** — [junglers](/wiki/glossary/jungler) (MY)
- **[Kramm](/wiki/players/kramm)** — [EXP laner](/wiki/glossary/exp-laner) (PH)

Head coach: Arcadia (PH).

## What comes next

As Season 17 champions, SRG earned one of Malaysia's slots at the
**[MLBB Mid Season Cup (MSC) 2026](/wiki/tournaments/msc-2026)** in Paris,
the international championship held under the Esports World Cup.

## Frequently asked questions

### How many MPL Malaysia titles have SRG won?

Five, across Seasons 13 to 17 — a record streak of five consecutive
championships, the most in the league's history.

### Who won MVP in the Season 17 final?

SRG roamer [Yums](/wiki/players/yums) was named MVP of both the regular season
and the grand final.
