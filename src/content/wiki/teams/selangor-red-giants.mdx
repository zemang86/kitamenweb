## Who are the Selangor Red Giants?

**Selangor Red Giants (SRG)** are a Malaysian esports organisation best known
for their [Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang)
roster, competing at the top of
[MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia). SRG are a consistent domestic
title contender and a regular Malaysian representative at regional and
international MLBB events.

## Why they matter

For Malaysian fans, SRG are one of the marquee brands of the MPL era — a useful
anchor point for understanding the local competitive landscape and the
rivalries that define each season.
