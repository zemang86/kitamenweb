## Who are Eastern Wolves Gangz?

**Eastern Wolves Gangz (EWG)** are a Malaysian **[Garena Free Fire](/wiki/games/free-fire)**
team. At the **[Free Fire Malaysia Championship (FFMC)](/wiki/tournaments/free-fire-malaysia-championship)
2026 Spring** — Malaysia's national Free Fire league — they finished **3rd** (around
US$4,200), behind champions **[Team Vamos](/wiki/teams/team-vamos)** and runners-up
**[Anyone Can Dream](/wiki/teams/anyone-can-dream)**.

The FFMC 2026 Spring roster was **Akidd, Fuzan, Xyber, Kaing, Rapal** and **Rndy**.
