## Who are Invictus Gaming (MPL Malaysia)?

**Invictus Gaming** competed in **[MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia)** Season 17
under the well-known Chinese **Invictus Gaming (iG)** esports brand, fielding a
**Malaysia-based [MLBB](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang) roster**. The MLBB division was
formed in January 2026 ahead of the franchised season.

## Season 17 (2026)

Invictus Gaming reached the **upper-bracket final** before falling to eventual champions
**[Selangor Red Giants](/wiki/teams/selangor-red-giants)**, then lost the lower-bracket final
**0–4** to **[Team Vamos](/wiki/teams/team-vamos)**, finishing **3rd** in their debut
franchised season — the best result of the league's mid-table challengers.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is Invictus Gaming a Malaysian team?

The Invictus Gaming brand is Chinese, but its MPL Malaysia entry is a Malaysia-based MLBB
roster competing in the country's national league.

### How did Invictus Gaming finish in MPL Malaysia Season 17?

3rd place, after reaching the upper-bracket final.
