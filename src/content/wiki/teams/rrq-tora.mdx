## Who are RRQ Tora?

**RRQ Tora** are the **Malaysian franchise** of the major Indonesian organisation **RRQ
(Rex Regum Qeon)**, one of Southeast Asia's best-known MLBB brands. They competed in the
franchised **[MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia)** Season 17 for
**[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang)** under the RRQ banner.

## Season 17 (2026)

RRQ Tora finished the regular season as the **6th seed (6–8)** and placed in the **5th–6th**
range of the playoffs in their debut franchised campaign.
