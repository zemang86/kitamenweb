## Who are CRIT VIP?

**CRIT VIP** (CRIT.VIP) is the **[Honor of Kings](/wiki/games/honor-of-kings)** squad of
**CRIT Esports**, a **Malaysian** org. It was a newcomer to the expanded 10-team
**[MY Honor of Kings League (MKL)](/wiki/tournaments/mkl)** Spring 2026, with a roster drawn
from CRIT's own **ACS EduTour** development circuit — an **all-Malaysian** up-and-coming lineup.

The Spring 2026 squad included **LeO**, **XiaoBa**, **Ze**, **ChloeY1**, **Lele** and
**M1NGyy** — all Malaysian.

## MKL Spring 2026

CRIT VIP finished **7th** in MKL Spring 2026 in their debut league season.
