## Who are Blepcat Gaming?

**Blepcat Gaming** competed in the
**[MY Honor of Kings League (MKL)](/wiki/tournaments/mkl)** Spring 2026 with a
**Malaysian-majority roster**. The team is a **rebrand of DrG** (announced around February
2026). Its corporate HQ is **not independently verified** — there is no dedicated Liquipedia
organisation page — so it sits in this wiki on the strength of its Malaysian-majority MKL roster
rather than a confirmed Malaysian org status.

The Spring 2026 lineup was **Bread** (top, MY), **Jason** (jungle, MY), **Louis** (mid, MY),
**Sober** (bot, CN) and **Elson** (roamer, MY) — four Malaysians and one Chinese import.

## MKL Spring 2026

Blepcat Gaming finished **9th** in MKL Spring 2026.
