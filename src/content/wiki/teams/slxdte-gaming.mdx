## Who are SLxDTE Gaming?

**SLxDTE Gaming** is a **Malaysian** team competing in the
**[MY Honor of Kings League (MKL)](/wiki/tournaments/mkl)**. The org is the rebranded
**DTE Gaming** (formerly **Diamond Teenagers Esports**), the new name following a January 2026
partnership with **SHENLING Esports** — the "SL" in SLxDTE. They reached Spring 2026 by winning
the **Honor of Kings Open Series Split 5 – Malaysia** qualifier.

The Spring 2026 lineup was **Xuann** (top, MY), **Blue** (jungle, CN), **Cece** (mid, MY),
**Hao** (bot, MY) and **Zero** (roamer, MY) — four Malaysians and one Chinese import.

## MKL Spring 2026

SLxDTE Gaming reached the playoffs and finished **4th**, behind champions
**[All Gamers Global](/wiki/teams/all-gamers-global)**, runners-up
**[Geekay Esports](/wiki/teams/geekay-esports)** and third-placed
**[Nova by ROC Esports](/wiki/teams/nova-by-roc-esports)**.
