## Who are XDG Esports?

**XDG Esports** (formerly Team XDG) is a **Malaysian** grassroots
**[Honor of Kings](/wiki/games/honor-of-kings)** team that earned its first
**[MY Honor of Kings League (MKL)](/wiki/tournaments/mkl)** appearance through qualification,
finishing 4th in the **Honor of Kings Open Series 2026 Split 5 – Malaysia**. Its starting five
are all Malaysian.

The Spring 2026 lineup was **Fahui** (top), **Seventeen** (jungle), **Xiaoyu** (mid),
**Redamancy** (bot) and **Rain** (roamer) — all Malaysian.

## MKL Spring 2026

XDG Esports finished **8th** in MKL Spring 2026 as a first-time league participant. Their
nationality rests on an all-Malaysian roster and the domestic qualifier path — XDG does not
have a dedicated organisation page on Liquipedia.
