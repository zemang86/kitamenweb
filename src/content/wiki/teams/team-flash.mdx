## Who are Team Flash (MPL Malaysia)?

**Team Flash** are a Southeast Asian esports organisation of **Singaporean origin** that
moved across from MPL Singapore to join **[MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia)**
as a franchise slot for Season 17, competing in
**[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang)** with a SEA roster
spanning Singaporean, Malaysian and Filipino players.

## Season 17 (2026)

In their first franchised MPL Malaysia season, Team Flash finished **8th** (1–13 in the
regular season) — bottom of the eight-team field.
