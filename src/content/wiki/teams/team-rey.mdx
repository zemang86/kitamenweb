## Who are Team Rey?

**Team Rey** are a **Malaysian** esports organisation — formerly **Barracuda Esports** — that
competed in the franchised **[MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia)** Season 17 for
**[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang)**. They are one of the
genuinely home-grown orgs in the franchise alongside
**[Selangor Red Giants](/wiki/teams/selangor-red-giants)** and
**[Team Vamos](/wiki/teams/team-vamos)**.

## Season 17 (2026)

Team Rey were a competitive mid-table side, taking the **4th seed (8–6)** out of the regular
season before finishing in the **5th–6th** range of the playoffs — ahead of fellow
franchise side RRQ Tora on regular-season seeding.

## Frequently asked questions

### Was Team Rey always called Team Rey?

No — the Malaysian organisation was formerly known as Barracuda Esports.

### How did Team Rey finish in MPL Malaysia Season 17?

In the 5th–6th range, having qualified for the playoffs as the 4th regular-season seed.
