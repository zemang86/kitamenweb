## Who are Geekay Esports?

**Geekay Esports** are a **UAE (Emirati)** esports organisation that fields a **Malaysian
[Honor of Kings](/wiki/games/honor-of-kings) roster**. In January 2026 they acquired the
Malaysian championship core of **[Homebois / Black Shrew (HBSE)](/wiki/teams/homebois)** — the
KIC 2025 world-podium lineage — to compete in the
**[MY Honor of Kings League (MKL)](/wiki/tournaments/mkl)**.

The MKL Spring 2026 roster was **GuiYu** (top), **MusangKing** (jungle), **Shisan** (mid),
**Jem** (bot) and **Zhe** (roamer).

## MKL Spring 2026

Geekay finished **runner-up** to **[All Gamers Global](/wiki/teams/all-gamers-global)**, and —
after the champions were ruled ineligible — took the **Honor of Kings Challenger Cup** slot,
carrying the ex-Homebois Malaysian core onto the regional stage.
