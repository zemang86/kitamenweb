## Who are Maqna Esports Club?

**Maqna Esports Club** is a **Malaysian** team competing in the
**[MY Honor of Kings League (MKL)](/wiki/tournaments/mkl)** as a **partner team**, having been
promoted from the Open Series. Its roster is **all Malaysian**.

The Spring 2026 squad included **Yu**, **HuaHua**, **Klow**, **Ddou**, **Milo**, **MEE2** and
**Frain** — all Malaysian.

## MKL Spring 2026

Maqna Esports Club finished **10th** in MKL Spring 2026. The roster also reached the **Mountain
Dew Challenge 2026** qualifier rounds.
