## Who are Team HAQ?

**Team HAQ** is a **Malaysian** team competing in the
**[MY Honor of Kings League (MKL)](/wiki/tournaments/mkl)** as a **partner team**, having
debuted at KIS4 ahead of the Spring 2026 season. Its starting five are **all Malaysian**.

The Spring 2026 lineup was **BAI** (top), **Mannn** (jungle), **ISZ** (mid), **WenHao** (bot)
and **NuoNuo** (roamer) — all Malaysian.

## MKL Spring 2026

Team HAQ finished **5th** in MKL Spring 2026. The roster also reached the **HoK Invitational
2026 Season 4**, falling to **[Nova by ROC Esports](/wiki/teams/nova-by-roc-esports)**.
