## Who are Twisted Minds?

**Twisted Minds** is a **Saudi Arabian** esports organisation, founded in 2021 and based in
**Riyadh**, with teams across many titles (PUBG, Warzone, Overwatch, EA Sports FC, Honor of
Kings). It is **not a Malaysian org** — but it belongs in this wiki because of how it entered
Malaysia's national **[Honor of Kings](/wiki/games/honor-of-kings)** league.

After its earlier European Honor of Kings squad (the ex-Vitality lineup) departed in February
2026, Twisted Minds signed a **Malaysian roster** specifically to compete in the
**[MY Honor of Kings League (MKL)](/wiki/tournaments/mkl)** Spring 2026.

The MKL lineup was **K1** (top, MY), **Clean** (jungle, CN), **Care** (mid, MY), **X1** (bot, MY)
and **Anda** (roamer, MY) — four Malaysians and one Chinese import.

## MKL Spring 2026

Twisted Minds finished **6th** in MKL Spring 2026 — the strongest result among the league's
mid-table sides that season. Separately, the org has competed at the international level,
including the **[Honor of Kings World Cup](/wiki/tournaments/honor-of-kings-world-cup-2026)**
cycle, though those are organisation achievements rather than Malaysian ones.
