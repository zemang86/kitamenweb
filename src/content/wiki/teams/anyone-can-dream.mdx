## Who are Anyone Can Dream?

**Anyone Can Dream** are a Malaysian **[Garena Free Fire](/wiki/games/free-fire)** team. They
finished **runner-up** at the
**[Free Fire Malaysia Championship (FFMC)](/wiki/tournaments/free-fire-malaysia-championship)
2026 Spring** (around US$10,450), behind champions
**[Team Vamos](/wiki/teams/team-vamos)** and ahead of third-placed
**[Eastern Wolves Gangz](/wiki/teams/eastern-wolves-gangz)**.
