## Who are Nova by ROC Esports?

**Nova by ROC Esports** competed in the
**[MY Honor of Kings League (MKL)](/wiki/tournaments/mkl)** Spring 2026 — the international
**Nova** brand (headquartered in Hong Kong/China) fielding a **Malaysian-majority roster** via
a partnership with **ROC Esports**, formed in February 2026.

The Spring 2026 lineup was **Xuan** (top), **KongLing** (jungle), **Wendy** (mid, CN),
**Muci** (bot) and **Weipit** (roamer).

## MKL Spring 2026

Nova by ROC Esports finished **3rd** in MKL Spring 2026, behind champions
**[All Gamers Global](/wiki/teams/all-gamers-global)** and runners-up
**[Geekay Esports](/wiki/teams/geekay-esports)** — a strong result for a first-season
partnership side.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is Nova by ROC Esports a Malaysian team?

It is the international Nova brand fielding a Malaysian-majority roster in Malaysia's national
MKL, via a partnership with ROC Esports — a Malaysian-rostered MKL competitor rather than a
Malaysian-owned org.

### How did Nova by ROC Esports finish in MKL Spring 2026?

3rd place.
