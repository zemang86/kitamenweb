## Who are Bigetron MY by VIT?

**Bigetron MY by VIT** are the **Malaysian franchise** of the Indonesian organisation
**Bigetron by Vitality**, competing in
**[MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia)** for
**[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang)**. The Malaysia-based
MLBB team joined the franchised league in March 2026.

## Season 17 (2026)

In their debut franchised season, Bigetron MY by VIT finished **4th**, eliminated in the
lower bracket by runners-up **[Team Vamos](/wiki/teams/team-vamos)** — a solid first showing
for the new Malaysian franchise.
