## Who are Team Vamos?

**Team Vamos** are a Malaysian **multi-title esports organisation** founded by
Mohd Faris. They field a leading
[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang) franchise in
[MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia) and a championship-winning
[Garena Free Fire](/wiki/games/free-fire) roster — making them one of the few MY
orgs competing at the top of two different titles.

## Season 17 (2026)

Team Vamos reached the **MPL Malaysia Season 17** grand final, where they lost
**0–4** to [Selangor Red Giants](/wiki/teams/selangor-red-giants) on
**7 June 2026** at Stadium Juara, Bukit Kiara. It was their **second** MPL
Malaysia final, after also finishing runner-up in Season 14.

## What comes next

As Season 17 runner-up, Team Vamos earned one of Malaysia's two slots at the
**[MLBB Mid Season Cup (MSC) 2026](/wiki/tournaments/msc-2026)** in Paris,
joining champions SRG to represent the country under the Esports World Cup.

## Free Fire division

Team Vamos also compete in **[Garena Free Fire](/wiki/games/free-fire)**, where
they are one of Malaysia's strongest sides. They **won the
[Free Fire Malaysia Championship (FFMC) 2026 Spring](/wiki/tournaments/free-fire-malaysia-championship)**
(9–25 January 2026, US$50,000 prize pool), earning a Southeast Asia qualifier
slot toward the
**[Free Fire World Series](/wiki/tournaments/free-fire-world-series)** circuit.

Runner-up — they lost 0–4 to Selangor Red Giants in the grand final on
