## Who are All Gamers Global?

**All Gamers Global** were the champions of the
**[MY Honor of Kings League (MKL)](/wiki/tournaments/mkl)** Spring 2026 — the third edition
of Malaysia's national **[Honor of Kings](/wiki/games/honor-of-kings)** league. They are a
**Malaysia-based team** under the Chinese **"All Gamers" (AG)** esports brand, with a
**Malaysian-majority roster**.

The Spring 2026 lineup was **Siang** (top, MY), **YuLong** (jungle, CN), **ZhiHong** (mid,
MY), **JR** (bot, MY) and **Saber** (roamer, MY).

## MKL Spring 2026 champions

All Gamers Global won the **MKL Spring 2026** title (US$16,400). However, because of ownership
ties to the Chinese AG club, they were ruled ineligible for the downstream Honor of Kings
Challenger Cup, so that slot passed to runners-up **[Geekay Esports](/wiki/teams/geekay-esports)**.
The roster later moved on to Aurora Gaming.

> The brand should not be confused with **AG Super Play**, the Chinese AG team that plays the
> King Pro League (KPL) in China. All Gamers Global is the AG brand's Malaysia-based side.
