## Who are Homebois?

**Homebois** are a Malaysian multi-title esports organisation, with teams in both
**[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang)** and
**[Honor of Kings](/wiki/games/honor-of-kings)**.

## Homebois in Mobile Legends

Homebois' MLBB team won **[MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia) Season 12 (2023)** —
with **[Xorn](/wiki/players/xorn)** named Finals MVP — and were runners-up in Seasons 13 and 14.
That championship core included **[Chibi](/wiki/players/chibi)** and
**[Sepat](/wiki/players/sepat)**, making Homebois one of the strongest Malaysian MLBB sides of
the era.

## Homebois x Black Shrew Esport (HBSE) — Honor of Kings world champions

In **August 2025** the Malaysian team **Black Shrew Esport** partnered with Homebois to compete
in Honor of Kings as **HomeBois BSE (HBSE)**. Under that banner the roster won the
**[Honor of Kings International Championship (KIC) 2025](/wiki/tournaments/kic-2025)** in Manila,
defeating **Nova Esports 4–0** in the grand final — a back-to-back world title, headlining an
all-Malaysian podium.

The HomeBois × Black Shrew partnership **ended on 31 December 2025**, and the championship core
moved on to **[Geekay Esports](/wiki/teams/geekay-esports)** for the 2026 season, so "HBSE"
now refers to that title-winning lineup of late 2025 rather than an active team.
