## Who are Homebois x Black Shrew Esport?

**Homebois x Black Shrew Esport (HBSE)** was a Malaysian
[Honor of Kings](/wiki/games/honor-of-kings) roster, formed when the Malaysian
team **Black Shrew Esport** partnered with **HomeBois** in **August 2025** and
competed as *HomeBois BSE*.

## Back-to-back world champions

Under that banner, the roster won the
**[Honor of Kings International Championship (KIC) 2025](/wiki/tournaments/kic-2025)**
in Manila, defeating **Nova Esports 4–0** in the grand final — successfully
**defending** their world title for a second straight championship. The win
headlined an all-Malaysian podium.

## What happened after

The HomeBois × Black Shrew partnership **ended on 31 December 2025**. The core of
the championship roster has since continued to compete under a different banner
(reported as **Geekay Esports**) for the 2026 season, so "HomeBois BSE" now
refers to the title-winning lineup of late 2025 rather than an active team.
