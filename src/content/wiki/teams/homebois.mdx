## Who are Homebois x Black Shrew Esport?

**Homebois x Black Shrew Esport (HBSE)** are a Malaysian esports organisation and
the reigning world champions of [Honor of Kings](/wiki/games/honor-of-kings).

## Back-to-back world champions

HBSE won the **[Honor of Kings International Championship (KIC) 2025](/wiki/tournaments/kic-2025)**
in Manila, defeating **Nova Esports 4–0** in the grand final — their **second
straight** KIC world title. The win headlined an all-Malaysian podium and made
HBSE the team to beat heading into the 2026 international season.

## Why they matter

HBSE are the strongest evidence that Malaysia is a top
[Honor of Kings](/wiki/games/honor-of-kings) nation: a Malaysian organisation
holds the game's world championship two years running.

## Frequently asked questions

### What is HBSE known for?

Winning back-to-back Honor of Kings International Championship titles (2024 and
2025) as a Malaysian organisation.

### Who did HBSE beat to win KIC 2025?

Nova Esports, 4–0, in the best-of-seven grand final in Manila.
