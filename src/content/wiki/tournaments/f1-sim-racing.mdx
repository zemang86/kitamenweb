## What is F1 Sim Racing?

**F1 Sim Racing** is the **official Formula 1 esport**, played on EA's
**[F1 game](/wiki/games/f1)**. Renamed from the **Formula One Esports Series** (from the
2023-24 cycle), it features **teams tied to the real F1 grid** — Red Bull, Ferrari, Mercedes
and others field virtual line-ups that contest a season mirroring real F1.

## 2025 champions

The **2025 championship** — contested on the F1 24 game — was won by **Jarno Opmeer
(Netherlands)** for **Oracle Red Bull Sim Racing**. It was Opmeer's **record third** drivers'
title (after 2020 and 2021), and Red Bull took the constructors' championship.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is F1 Sim Racing official?

Yes — it is Formula 1's official esport, run with EA Sports and Codemasters, with teams
attached to the real F1 grid.

### Who has won the most F1 Sim Racing titles?

Jarno Opmeer, a three-time champion (2020, 2021 and 2025).
