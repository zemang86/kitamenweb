## What is the PGL Major Singapore 2026?

The **PGL Major Singapore 2026** is a **Valve-sponsored Counter-Strike Major** — the
flagship tier of **[Counter-Strike 2](/wiki/games/counter-strike-2)** — organised by
**PGL** at the **Singapore Indoor Stadium**, **25 November – 13 December 2026**. It brings
together **32 teams** for a **US$1,250,000** prize pool.

## Why it matters for the region

It is the **first-ever Counter-Strike Major held in Southeast Asia** — a milestone for a
region that has long been minor in CS. For Malaysian and SEA fans it is the closest a
CS Major has ever come to home, and the strongest validation yet of the region's growing
CS2 infrastructure.

The 2026 season's first Major, **IEM Cologne 2026**, was won by **Team Falcons**.

## Frequently asked questions

### Why is the PGL Major Singapore 2026 significant?

It is the first Counter-Strike Major ever staged in Southeast Asia, bringing the game's
most prestigious event to the region for the first time.

### Is there a Malaysian team at the Major?

The Major is a global event reached through international qualifiers; as of 2026 there is no
top-tier Malaysian CS2 team in contention. Malaysia's best-known CS export, kaze, has
competed internationally with China-based orgs.
