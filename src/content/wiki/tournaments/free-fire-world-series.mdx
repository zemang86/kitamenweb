## What is the Free Fire World Series?

The **Free Fire World Series (FFWS)** is **[Garena Free Fire's](/wiki/games/free-fire)**
top global championship, gathering the best teams from regional leagues around the
world. It is the pinnacle event Malaysian teams aim for through the
**[Free Fire Malaysia Championship](/wiki/tournaments/free-fire-malaysia-championship)**
and the Southeast Asia circuit.

## FFWS 2025 Global Finals

The most recent global edition, the **FFWS 2025 Global Finals**, was held at the
**Indonesia Arena in Jakarta** from **31 October to 15 November 2025**, with a
**US$1,000,000** prize pool. **Buriram United Esports** of Thailand won the
Battle Royale title and the US$300,000 top prize.

## Malaysia at the FFWS

Malaysian teams reach the FFWS through the regional **Southeast Asia** tier rather
than directly — no Malaysia-based org featured in the 2025 Global Finals field.
Domestically, **[Team Vamos](/wiki/teams/team-vamos)** earned a Southeast Asia
qualifier slot by winning the
**[FFMC 2026 Spring](/wiki/tournaments/free-fire-malaysia-championship)** national
title.

## Frequently asked questions

### Who won the Free Fire World Series 2025?

Buriram United Esports of Thailand won the Battle Royale title at the FFWS 2025
Global Finals in Jakarta, taking the US$300,000 first prize from a US$1 million
pool.

### How do Malaysian teams qualify for the FFWS?

By winning or placing highly in the
[Free Fire Malaysia Championship](/wiki/tournaments/free-fire-malaysia-championship),
which feeds the regional Southeast Asia stage that leads to the world finals.
