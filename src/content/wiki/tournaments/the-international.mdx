## What is The International?

**The International (TI)** is **Valve's** annual **[Dota 2](/wiki/games/dota-2)** world
championship — the sport's most prestigious event. The winning team lifts the
**[Aegis of Champions](/wiki/glossary/aegis-of-champions)**. For years TI prize pools were
crowdfunded through in-game Battle Pass sales, peaking at the all-time esports record of
**~US$40 million at TI 2021**; pools have since shrunk after Valve retired the Battle Pass.

Recent winners: **Team Liquid (TI 2024)** and **Team Falcons (TI 2025)**.

## Malaysia at The International

Malaysia's landmark TI result is
**[Orange eSports' 3rd-place finish at TI3 (2013)](/wiki/teams/orange-esports)** — then the
best-ever placement for a Southeast Asian team. That all-Malaysian roster featured
**[Mushi](/wiki/players/mushi)** and led into the **Team Malaysia → Fnatic** era. Later,
**[MidOne](/wiki/players/midone)** reached **4th at TI 2019** with Team Secret.

## Frequently asked questions

### What does the winner of The International receive?

The Aegis of Champions — a physical shield trophy — along with the lion's share of the
prize pool.

### What is Malaysia's best result at The International?

Orange eSports' 3rd place at TI3 (2013), a landmark for Malaysian and Southeast Asian
Dota 2.
