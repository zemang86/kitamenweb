## What is the MLBB Mid Season Cup?

The **MLBB Mid Season Cup (MSC)** is the international mid-season championship for
[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang). The rebranded
event replaces the former MLBB Southeast Asia Cup and now draws teams from across
the globe.

## MSC 2026 in Paris

The **2026 edition** runs from **1 July to 1 August 2026** at the **Paris Expo
Porte de Versailles**, France — held as part of the **Esports World Cup 2026**.
**25 teams** compete for a **US$3 million** prize pool. (The event was originally
slated for Riyadh before being moved to Paris in May 2026.)

## Malaysia at MSC 2026

Malaysia's slots came through [MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia):
Season 17 champions [Selangor Red Giants](/wiki/teams/selangor-red-giants) and
runners-up Team Vamos both qualified.

## Frequently asked questions

### Where is MSC 2026 held?

At the Paris Expo Porte de Versailles in Paris, France, from 1 July to 1 August
2026, under the Esports World Cup.

### What is the MSC 2026 prize pool?

US$3 million, contested by 25 teams.
