## What is the Toyota GR GT Cup Malaysia?

The **Toyota GR GT Cup Malaysia** (the Toyota GR Malaysia eRacing Championship) is widely
described as **Malaysia's premier sim racing series**. Played on
**[Gran Turismo](/wiki/games/gran-turismo)** and run by **Toyota / Gazoo Racing Malaysia**,
it offers a clear competitive pathway: a **national** championship feeding an **Asia
regional** round and then a **World Finals**.

## A real talent pipeline

The series is part of Toyota's broader motorsport ladder, connecting sim racing to real-world
GR competition. **[Taj Izrin Aiman](/wiki/players/taj-izrin-aiman)** won the **GR GT Cup Asia
2024** individual title and went on to represent Asia at the GR GT Cup Global Finals — one of
the clearest examples of Malaysian sim racing producing internationally competitive drivers.
