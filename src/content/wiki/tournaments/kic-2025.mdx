## What was KIC 2025?

The **Honor of Kings International Championship (KIC) 2025** was the global
world championship for [Honor of Kings](/wiki/games/honor-of-kings), held in
**Manila, Philippines** from **14–30 November 2025** with a **US$1 million**
prize pool.

## An all-Malaysian podium

Malaysian teams took **all three podium places**. In the best-of-seven grand
final, **[Homebois x Black Shrew Esport (HBSE)](/wiki/teams/homebois)** beat
**Nova Esports 4–0** to claim the world title — their second straight KIC
championship. Star player **GuiYu** was named Finals MVP.

The final pushed KIC 2025 to a concurrent viewership peak of around **694,000**,
the highest for any Honor of Kings event held outside China that year.

## Why it matters for Malaysia

KIC 2025 is the clearest signal that Malaysia is a genuine
[Honor of Kings](/wiki/games/honor-of-kings) powerhouse, not just an MLBB
nation — a Malaysian organisation is the reigning HOK world champion.
