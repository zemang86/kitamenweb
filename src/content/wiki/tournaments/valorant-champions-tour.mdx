## What is the VALORANT Champions Tour?

The **VALORANT Champions Tour (VCT)** is **[VALORANT's](/wiki/games/valorant)**
global esports circuit, run by **Riot Games**. It is built around franchised
**international leagues** whose seasons feed two annual **Masters** events and the
season-ending world final, **VALORANT Champions**.

## VCT 2026 structure

In **2026** the VCT runs **four international leagues** — **Americas, EMEA,
Pacific and China** — with **12 teams each** (48 total). The season flows from
Kickoff through Masters (Santiago, then London) and two stages into **VALORANT
Champions 2026 in Shanghai**, with each region sending four teams to the world
final.

> 2026 is reported to be the final franchised season, with the VCT planned to
> return to an open-qualifier system from 2027 — a forward-looking plan that may
> still change.

## VALORANT Champions 2025

The most recent world champions are **NRG** (Americas), who beat **Fnatic**
(EMEA) **3–2** in the grand final in **Paris** — NRG's first VALORANT Champions
title.

## Where Malaysia fits

Malaysia competes within the Southeast Asia region under
**[VCT Pacific](/wiki/tournaments/vct-pacific)**, the international league for the
Asia-Pacific.
