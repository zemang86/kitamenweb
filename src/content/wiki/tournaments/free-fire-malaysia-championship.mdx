## What is the FFMC?

The **Free Fire Malaysia Championship (FFMC)** is Malaysia's national league for
**[Garena Free Fire](/wiki/games/free-fire)**, organised by Garena. It is the top
domestic competition, run on a seasonal **Spring / Fall** structure, and the path
through which Malaysian teams reach the regional Southeast Asia stage and the
**[Free Fire World Series](/wiki/tournaments/free-fire-world-series)**.

> Naming note: the FFMC is the current official name of Malaysia's top-tier Free
> Fire competition. Older "Free Fire Master League (FFML)" references are outdated.

## FFMC 2026 Spring

The **2026 Spring** season ran **9–25 January 2026** with **18 teams** and a
**US$50,000** prize pool. **[Team Vamos](/wiki/teams/team-vamos)** were crowned
champions, securing a **Southeast Asia qualifier slot** toward the FFWS 2026
circuit. **[Anyone Can Dream](/wiki/teams/anyone-can-dream)** finished runner-up and
**[Eastern Wolves Gangz (EWG)](/wiki/teams/eastern-wolves-gangz)** took third.

A **2026 Fall** edition follows later in the year; results were not yet final at
the time of writing.
