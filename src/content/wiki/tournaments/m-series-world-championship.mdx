## What is the M-Series?

The **M-Series World Championship** is the annual world championship for
[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang), run by Moonton.
It is the sport's most prestigious title — the event every regional league,
including [MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia), ultimately aims to reach.

## Recent champions

- **M7 (January 2026, Jakarta)** — **Aurora Gaming PH** beat Alter Ego **4–0**,
  with roamer Light named Finals MVP, from a US$1 million prize pool.
- **M6 (December 2024)** — **Fnatic ONIC PH** beat Team Liquid **4–1**.

Both results extended the **Philippines'** unbroken run of M-Series world titles.

## Malaysia at the M-Series

Malaysia's berths come through [MPL Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/mpl-malaysia). As
the most decorated MPL MY franchise,
[Selangor Red Giants](/wiki/teams/selangor-red-giants) are the country's most
frequent representatives on the world stage.

## Frequently asked questions

### Who won the latest M-Series?

Aurora Gaming PH won M7 in January 2026, sweeping Alter Ego 4–0 in Jakarta.

### How do Malaysian teams qualify for the M-Series?

Through MPL Malaysia — the league's top finishers earn Malaysia's slots at the
world championship.
