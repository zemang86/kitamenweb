## What is the Honor of Kings World Cup?

The **Honor of Kings World Cup 2026** — branded **"KWC at EWC 26"** — is the
international championship for [Honor of Kings](/wiki/games/honor-of-kings), held
as part of the **Esports World Cup 2026** in **Paris, France**, from **30 July to
8 August 2026**. It carries a **US$3 million** prize pool contested by **20 teams**.

For its EWC run, Honor of Kings is paired with **Arena of Valor** — the first
midseason championship to combine both games' teams and heroes into one shared
competitive patch.

## Malaysia at the World Cup

Malaysian teams reach the World Cup through the
**[MY Honor of Kings League (MKL)](/wiki/tournaments/mkl)** and the wider regional
circuit. Spring 2026 MKL champions **All Gamers Global** earned a direct
qualification slot.

## Defending champion

**AG.AL** won the 2025 edition at the Esports World Cup, completing a 4–3 reverse
sweep in the grand final in Paris.

Through the MY Honor of Kings League and the regional Honor of Kings circuit; the
