## What is the PMGC?

The **PUBG Mobile Global Championship (PMGC)** is the **year-end world finals** of
**[PUBG Mobile](/wiki/games/pubg-mobile)** esports, organised by **Level Infinite** and
**KRAFTON**. It gathers the best teams from every region after a season of national and
regional play, scored by combining **[placement points](/wiki/glossary/placement-points)**
with eliminations — a single match win being a **[WWCD](/wiki/glossary/wwcd)**.

A second global pinnacle, the **PUBG Mobile World Cup**, is staged within the **Esports
World Cup** in Riyadh.

## Recent champions

- **PMGC 2025** — **Alpha7 Esports (Brazil)**, in Bangkok (US$3M prize pool); the first
  Brazilian org to win it.
- **PUBG Mobile World Cup 2025** — **Yangon Galacticos (Myanmar)**, at the Esports World
  Cup (US$3M prize pool).

## The Malaysian pathway

Malaysian teams reach global events through **PMNC Malaysia** (national) and the regional
**PMPL** (Southeast Asia) — the league tier briefly ran as **PMSL** in 2024–25 before
PMPL's 2026 return. **PMPL Malaysia 2026 Spring** was won by **Maqna Esports**, and
**Vampire Esports** placed **2nd** at the regional PMSL SEA Fall 2025 — the strongest
recent Malaysian result on the road to the PMGC.

## Frequently asked questions

### Who won the PMGC 2025?

Alpha7 Esports of Brazil, in Bangkok — the first Brazilian organisation to win the PUBG
Mobile Global Championship.

### How do Malaysian teams qualify for the PMGC?

Through the national PMNC Malaysia and the regional PMPL (Southeast Asia) circuit, which
sends its top teams to the global finals.
