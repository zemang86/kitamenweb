## What is MPL Malaysia?

**MPL Malaysia** is the **Mobile Legends: Bang Bang Professional League** for
Malaysia — the top-tier domestic competition for
[MLBB](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang). It runs as a franchised league:
a fixed set of partnered teams play a regular season, then the top teams
advance to playoffs to crown a champion and earn slots at international events.

## How the season works

A typical MPL season has two phases:

- **Regular season** — round-robin matches that seed the standings.
- **Playoffs** — a bracket where the regular-season leaders compete for the
  Immortal Cup and qualification to regional/international events.

## The franchise era (2026)

Season 17 marked MPL Malaysia's shift to a **partnership/franchise model**,
launched by Moonton with the Malaysia Esports Federation (MESF) and Esports
Integrated (ESI). The eight franchised teams were:
[Selangor Red Giants](/wiki/teams/selangor-red-giants),
[Team Vamos](/wiki/teams/team-vamos), Invictus Gaming, Bigetron MY by VIT,
RRQ Tora, Team Rey, AC Esports and Team Flash.

## Season 17 (2026)

[Selangor Red Giants](/wiki/teams/selangor-red-giants) won **Season 17**,
beating **Team Vamos 4–0** in the grand final on **7 June 2026** at Stadium
Juara, Bukit Kiara — their record fifth consecutive title. The season carried a
**US$100,000** prize pool, with the champions taking roughly **US$31,900**. SRG
and Vamos claimed Malaysia's two slots at the
**[MLBB Mid Season Cup (MSC) 2026](/wiki/tournaments/msc-2026)** in Paris.

## Frequently asked questions

### Who won MPL Malaysia Season 17?

Selangor Red Giants, who defeated Team Vamos 4–0 in the grand final on 7 June

### How do teams qualify for international events from MPL Malaysia?

The top finishers each season earn Malaysia's slots at international MLBB
