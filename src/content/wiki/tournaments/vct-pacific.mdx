## What is VCT Pacific?

**VCT Pacific** is the **Asia-Pacific** international league of the
**[VALORANT Champions Tour](/wiki/tournaments/valorant-champions-tour)** — one of
the four tier-1 leagues of professional
**[VALORANT](/wiki/games/valorant)**. It is the league Malaysian players reach via
the Southeast Asia pathway, and it is home to **Paper Rex**, whose in-game leader
**[d4v41](/wiki/players/d4v41)** is Malaysian.

## The Malaysian / SEA pathway (2026)

Malaysia does **not** have a standalone "Challengers Malaysia." Instead, Malaysian
teams compete in **VALORANT Challengers Southeast Asia (Challengers SEA)** — the
regional tier-2 circuit.

For **2026**, Riot **removed the Ascension promotion path**. Top Challengers SEA
teams now earn **direct slots into the international league's Stage 2 Playoffs**
(which feed VALORANT Champions): the Challengers SEA playoff champion advances to
VCT Pacific's Stage 2 stage, while the runner-up and the top Challenger-points team
go to a **Last Chance Qualifier (LCQ)** against South Asia and Oceania.

> Naming note: the current tier-2 circuit is **Challengers SEA**, not "Challengers
> Malaysia," and the old **Ascension** route no longer exists as of 2026.

## Frequently asked questions

### What league does Malaysia play in for VALORANT?

Malaysia is part of Southeast Asia, which competes in Challengers SEA and feeds
into VCT Pacific, the Asia-Pacific tier-1 league.

### Is there still a VCT Ascension in 2026?

No. Ascension was removed for 2026; top Challengers SEA teams now earn direct slots
into the international league's Stage 2 Playoffs instead.
