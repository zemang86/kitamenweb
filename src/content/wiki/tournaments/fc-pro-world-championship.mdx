## What is the FC Pro World Championship?

The **FC Pro World Championship** is the season-ending world final of **EA's** own
competitive program for **[EA SPORTS FC](/wiki/games/ea-sports-fc)**, **FC Pro** — the
circuit that replaced the old "FIFA Global Series" after EA and FIFA parted ways. Players
qualify through **open ladders → regional qualifiers → a global qualifier**, competing
**1v1** in the **[Ultimate Team (FUT)](/wiki/glossary/ultimate-team)** mode.

## 2026 edition

The **FC Pro 26 World Championship** runs in **Paris, 22–26 July 2026**, staged as part of
the **Esports World Cup 2026**. A flagship partner event on the road there, the
**eChampions League 2026** (UEFA-partnered), was won by **Anders Vejrgang** of Denmark.

## Where Malaysia fits

Malaysia competes in EA's **"Asia South"** regional bracket. No Malaysian has yet reached
the FC Pro World Championship — the confirmed 2026 field is led by European and North
African players — but **[Rajabos](/wiki/players/rajabos)** is the standout local name on
the FC Pro ladder, having taken an Asia South regional cup.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is the FC Pro World Championship the same as the FIFAe World Cup?

No. Since the 2022 split, EA runs the FC Pro circuit for EA SPORTS FC, while the separate
"FIFAe" brand runs Konami's eFootball and other titles. They are different competitions on
different games.

### Has a Malaysian reached the FC Pro World Championship?

Not as of 2026. Malaysian players compete in EA's Asia South region; the strongest local
result is a regional cup win by Rajabos.
