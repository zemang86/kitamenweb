## What is the MKL?

The **MY Honor of Kings League (MKL)** is Malaysia's national league for
[Honor of Kings](/wiki/games/honor-of-kings), operated by **Level Infinite**. It
is the top domestic competition that feeds Malaysian teams into the game's
international circuit.

## MKL Spring 2026

The **Spring 2026** season was the league's **third edition**, beginning in
February 2026 with **10 teams**.
**[All Gamers Global](/wiki/teams/all-gamers-global)** were crowned champions,
ahead of runners-up **[Geekay Esports](/wiki/teams/geekay-esports)** and
third-placed **[Nova by ROC Esports](/wiki/teams/nova-by-roc-esports)**. The season
carried a **US$60,000** prize pool.

Because of ownership ties to the Chinese AG club, the champions were ruled
ineligible for the downstream **Honor of Kings Challenger Cup**, so that slot passed
to **[Geekay Esports](/wiki/teams/geekay-esports)** — which fields the former
**Black Shrew Esport** (ex-[Homebois BSE](/wiki/teams/homebois)) Malaysian core,
after the previous champions were dropped from the partnership programme.

## How the MKL fits the global picture

In 2026 Honor of Kings restructured its ecosystem, with national leagues like the
MKL feeding into higher-tier regional and global competition and ultimately the
[Honor of Kings World Cup](/wiki/tournaments/honor-of-kings-world-cup-2026).

## Frequently asked questions

### Who won MKL Spring 2026?

All Gamers Global, ahead of runners-up Geekay Esports and third-placed Nova by ROC
Esports.

### Who runs the MY Honor of Kings League?

Level Infinite, Tencent's international publisher for Honor of Kings.
