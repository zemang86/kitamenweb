## What is the Gran Turismo World Series?

The **Gran Turismo World Series (GTWS)** is the official esport of
**[Gran Turismo](/wiki/games/gran-turismo)**, run by **Polyphony Digital** and **Sony**. It
began as the **FIA-Certified Gran Turismo Championships** in 2018 — the first FIA-sanctioned
digital motorsport — before the FIA branding was dropped from 2022.

It runs two formats: the **Nations Cup**, where drivers race for their **country**, and the
**Manufacturers Cup**, where they race for a **car brand**, culminating in an annual World
Finals.

## 2025 World Finals

At the **2025 World Finals in Fukuoka, Japan**, **José Serrano (Spain)** won the Nations Cup,
and **Porsche** took the Manufacturers Cup — the first non-Japanese manufacturer to win in
the series' recent history.

## Where Malaysia fits

Malaysia competes through the **Asia** region and is eligible for the Nations Cup; Sony runs a
Malaysia-specific GTWS competition each season. (No Malaysian has been verified as reaching a
GTWS World Finals to date.)

## Frequently asked questions

### What is the difference between Nations Cup and Manufacturers Cup?

In the Nations Cup you race for your country; in the Manufacturers Cup you race for a car
brand as part of its three-driver team.

### Was the Gran Turismo World Series ever FIA-sanctioned?

Yes — as the FIA-Certified Gran Turismo Championships from 2018 to 2021, the first
FIA-sanctioned digital motorsport tournament.
