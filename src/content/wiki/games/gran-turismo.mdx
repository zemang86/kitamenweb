## What is Gran Turismo?

**Gran Turismo** is the **PlayStation racing simulator** developed by **Polyphony Digital**
and published by **Sony**. The current title is **Gran Turismo 7 (2022)**, a **console
exclusive** (PS4/PS5) — unusual in a discipline otherwise dominated by PC sims.

## The Gran Turismo World Series

GT's flagship esport is the **[Gran Turismo World Series (GTWS)](/wiki/tournaments/gran-turismo-world-series)**,
which the FIA sanctioned from 2018. It runs two formats: the **Nations Cup** (you race for
your country) and the **Manufacturers Cup** (you race for a car brand). At the **2025 World
Finals** in Fukuoka, **José Serrano (Spain)** won the Nations Cup and **Porsche** took the
Manufacturers Cup.

## Gran Turismo in Malaysia

Malaysia competes in GTWS through the **Asia** region, and GT7 underpins the country's most
prominent local sim series, the
**[Toyota GR GT Cup Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/toyota-gr-gt-cup-malaysia)** — whose
**[Taj Izrin Aiman](/wiki/players/taj-izrin-aiman)** won the GR GT Cup Asia 2024 title.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is Gran Turismo on PC?

No — Gran Turismo is a PlayStation console exclusive (PS4/PS5), unlike PC-based sims such as
iRacing and Assetto Corsa.

### What is the Gran Turismo World Series?

GT's official esport, with a Nations Cup (race for your country) and a Manufacturers Cup
(race for a car brand), culminating in an annual World Finals.
