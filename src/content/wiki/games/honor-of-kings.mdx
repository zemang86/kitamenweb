## What is Honor of Kings?

**Honor of Kings (HOK)** is a 5v5 mobile MOBA developed by **TiMi Studio Group**
(Tencent) and published internationally by **Level Infinite**. It is the global
version of China's *Wangzhe Rongyao* (2015) and reached its **worldwide launch on
20 June 2024**, going on to win **Google Play's Best Game of 2024 in Southeast
Asia**.

## Malaysia is an HOK powerhouse

Malaysia is one of the strongest HOK nations in the world. At the
**[Honor of Kings International Championship (KIC) 2025](/wiki/tournaments/kic-2025)**
in Manila, **Malaysian teams swept the entire podium** — and
**[Homebois x Black Shrew Esport](/wiki/teams/homebois)** beat Nova Esports 4–0
to win the world title. Domestically, the scene is anchored by the **Malaysia
Honor of Kings League (MKL)**, run by Level Infinite.

## Honor of Kings at the Esports World Cup

HOK is an **Esports World Cup 2026** title — featured as **"Honor of Kings and
Arena of Valor" (the King's World Cup at EWC)** in Paris, **30 July – 8 August
2026**, with a **US$3 million** prize pool. The MKL is one of the qualifying
paths for Malaysian teams.

## Frequently asked questions

### How is Honor of Kings different from Mobile Legends?

Both are 5v5 mobile MOBAs with similar objectives. They differ in hero rosters,
mechanics, and publisher ecosystems — HOK is published by Tencent's Level
Infinite/TiMi, while [MLBB](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang) is published by
Moonton, the entrenched Southeast Asian market leader.

### Is Malaysia good at Honor of Kings?

Yes. Malaysian teams took all three podium places at KIC 2025, with
[Homebois x Black Shrew Esport](/wiki/teams/homebois) winning the world title.
