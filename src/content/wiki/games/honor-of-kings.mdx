## What is Honor of Kings?

**Honor of Kings (HOK)** is a 5v5 mobile MOBA developed by **TiMi Studio Group**,
part of Tencent. Long one of the highest-grossing mobile games in the world, it
expanded internationally under the *Honor of Kings* brand and has been building
a competitive presence across Southeast Asia, Malaysia included.

## Honor of Kings in Malaysia

The Malaysian HOK scene is younger than the
[MLBB](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang) ecosystem but growing quickly,
supported by official regional circuits and grassroots events. Its core loop —
lanes, jungle, objectives — will feel familiar to any MOBA player.

## Frequently asked questions

### How is Honor of Kings different from Mobile Legends?

Both are 5v5 mobile MOBAs with similar objectives. They differ in hero rosters,
mechanics, and publisher ecosystems — HOK is published by Tencent's TiMi, while
MLBB is published by Moonton.
