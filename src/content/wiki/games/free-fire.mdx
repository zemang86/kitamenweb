## What is Garena Free Fire?

**Garena Free Fire** is a **mobile battle royale** developed by **111dots Studio**
and published by **Garena**. A match drops up to **50 players** onto an island;
squads of up to four scavenge for weapons and survive a **shrinking safe zone**
until only one squad remains. It became one of the most-downloaded mobile games
in the world on the back of fast, mobile-first 10-minute matches.

Competitive Free Fire is played in two formats: the **Battle Royale** circuit
(multiple squads per lobby across several maps) and **Clash Squad**, a tighter
**4v4, best-of-7** mode built for head-to-head esports.

## Free Fire in Malaysia

Malaysia is an active Free Fire nation with its own national league, the
**[Free Fire Malaysia Championship (FFMC)](/wiki/tournaments/free-fire-malaysia-championship)**,
run by Garena. The FFMC feeds Malaysian teams into the regional Southeast Asia
circuit and ultimately the
**[Free Fire World Series (FFWS)](/wiki/tournaments/free-fire-world-series)**.
The **2026 Spring** national title went to
**[Team Vamos](/wiki/teams/team-vamos)**.

> Note: while the original Free Fire was banned in India in 2022 (later relaunched
> as a separate "Free Fire India" build in 2025), the game has remained fully
> operational in Malaysia and the wider Southeast Asia region throughout.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is Free Fire still active in Malaysia in 2026?

Yes. Malaysia runs a full national league (the FFMC) with seasonal Spring and
Fall editions feeding the regional and world circuit.

### What is the difference between Battle Royale and Clash Squad in Free Fire?

Battle Royale is the classic last-squad-standing mode with up to 50 players;
Clash Squad is a 4v4, best-of-7 round-based mode designed for direct competitive
matches.
