## What is Assetto Corsa Competizione?

**Assetto Corsa Competizione (ACC)** is the **GT-focused racing simulator** by Italian
studio **Kunos Simulazioni**, published by **505 Games** and released in **2018** on Unreal
Engine 4. It is the **official game of the GT World Challenge**, built around real **GT3 and
GT4** machinery, with detailed tyre, brake and
**[Balance of Performance](/wiki/glossary/balance-of-performance)** modelling.

It is the sequel-of-sorts to the broader, mod-friendly original **Assetto Corsa (2014)**.
A newer entry, **Assetto Corsa EVO**, entered Steam Early Access in January 2025 — but the
official GT World Challenge licence stays with ACC.

## Esports

ACC underpins **SRO's GT World Challenge Esports**, run by SRO Motorsports with Kunos — the
official competitive ladder for the real-world GT World Challenge series.

## Frequently asked questions

### What makes ACC different from the original Assetto Corsa?

ACC is narrower and more official — focused on GT3/GT4 racing under the GT World Challenge
licence — while the 2014 original is broader and heavily mod-friendly.

### What cars does ACC focus on?

Real GT3 and GT4 racing cars, with Balance of Performance applied to keep different models
competitive.
