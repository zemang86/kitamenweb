## What is EA SPORTS F1?

**EA SPORTS F1** is the **official Formula 1 video game**, developed by **Codemasters** and
published by **EA Sports**. The current title is **F1 25**, the official game of the 2025 F1
season, available on PC, PlayStation and Xbox. It is the closest the discipline gets to
recreating the real F1 calendar, cars and regulations.

## F1 Sim Racing

The official esport, **[F1 Sim Racing](/wiki/tournaments/f1-sim-racing)** (renamed from the
**Formula One Esports Series**), pits teams tied to the real F1 grid against each other. The
**2025 championship** — contested on the F1 24 game — was won by **Jarno Opmeer
(Netherlands)** for **Oracle Red Bull Sim Racing**, his record **third** title, with Red Bull
taking the constructors' crown.

## Frequently asked questions

### Who makes the official F1 game?

Codemasters, published under the EA Sports label. The current release is F1 25.

### Who is the most successful F1 Sim Racing driver?

Jarno Opmeer, a three-time champion (2020, 2021, 2025).
