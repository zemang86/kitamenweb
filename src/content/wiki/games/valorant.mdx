## What is VALORANT?

**VALORANT** is a **5v5 tactical hero shooter** developed and published by
**Riot Games**. Released on PC on **2 June 2020**, it blends precise gunplay with
**agents** — characters whose abilities create or deny space — and came to
**PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S in 2024**. Competitive rounds revolve around
attackers planting the **[Spike](/wiki/glossary/spike)** (the bomb) and defenders
stopping them.

Every agent fits one of four **roles**:
**[Duelist](/wiki/glossary/duelist)**,
**[Initiator](/wiki/glossary/initiator)**,
**[Controller](/wiki/glossary/controller)** and
**[Sentinel](/wiki/glossary/sentinel)**.

## VALORANT in Malaysia

Malaysia competes within the **Southeast Asia** region, which sits under
**[VCT Pacific](/wiki/tournaments/vct-pacific)** — one of the four international
leagues of the **[VALORANT Champions Tour](/wiki/tournaments/valorant-champions-tour)**.
The country's most accomplished pro is **[d4v41](/wiki/players/d4v41)**, the
Malaysian in-game leader of **Paper Rex** and the first Malaysian to play at a VCT
international event.

## Frequently asked questions

### What are the four roles in VALORANT?

Duelist (aggressive entry), Initiator (set up takes), Controller (smokes and space
control) and Sentinel (defensive anchor).

### How do Malaysian teams compete in VALORANT?

Through the Southeast Asia pathway — the regional Challengers SEA circuit feeding
into [VCT Pacific](/wiki/tournaments/vct-pacific) and ultimately VALORANT
Champions.
