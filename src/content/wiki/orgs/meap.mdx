## What is MEAP?

The **Malaysia E-sports Accelerator Program (MEAP)** is a
**[Ministry of Youth and Sports](/wiki/orgs/kbs)** programme that supports
Malaysian **athletes and teams competing internationally** — the high-performance
counterpart to the grassroots-focused
**[Dana E-Sukan KBS (DEK)](/wiki/orgs/dek)** grant.

## MEAP 2026 at a glance

- **Allocation:** **RM500,000** for 2026.
- **Launched:** **12 March 2026**, together with DEK.
- **Combined with DEK:** the two programmes total **RM1.5 million** for 2026.

Both are delivered through
**[Esports Integrated (ESI)](/wiki/orgs/esports-integrated)** under KBS.

## Frequently asked questions

### What does MEAP fund?

Malaysian esports athletes and teams competing at the international level.

### How is MEAP different from DEK?

MEAP backs international competition (high performance); DEK funds community
tournaments and digital content (grassroots).
