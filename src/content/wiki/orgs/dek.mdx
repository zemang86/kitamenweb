## What is Dana E-Sukan KBS?

**Dana E-Sukan KBS (DEK)** is the **[Ministry of Youth and Sports'](/wiki/orgs/kbs)**
dedicated **esports grant fund**, administered by
**[Esports Integrated (ESI)](/wiki/orgs/esports-integrated)**. It funds two kinds
of work: **community tournaments / events** and **digital content** that grows the
local scene, with a focus on youth, inclusivity and rural reach.

## DEK 2026 at a glance

- **Fund size:** RM1 million for 2026.
- **Per project:** between **RM5,000 and RM20,000** (RM20k is the per-project cap,
  not a total).
- **Application window:** **12 March – 26 April 2026** (now closed).
- **Target:** **75+ projects** reaching around **22,000 youth**.

DEK runs alongside the **[MEAP](/wiki/orgs/meap)** accelerator; the two were
allocated **RM1.5 million** combined for 2026.

## Frequently asked questions

### How much can a project get from DEK?

Between RM5,000 and RM20,000 per project — RM20,000 is the per-project cap.

### Who administers Dana E-Sukan KBS?

Esports Integrated (ESI), on behalf of the Ministry of Youth and Sports.
