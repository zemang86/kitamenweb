## What is KBS?

**Kementerian Belia dan Sukan (KBS)** — the **Ministry of Youth and Sports** — is
the Malaysian government ministry responsible for youth development and sport,
including **esports**. Its current minister is **Dr. Mohammed Taufiq Johari**,
appointed in the December 2025 cabinet reshuffle.

## KBS and esports

Malaysia formally **recognised esports as a sport in 2023** (via an amendment to
the Sports Development Act 1997), and KBS has since led the government's esports
agenda. It delivers funding through its implementing agency
**[Esports Integrated (ESI)](/wiki/orgs/esports-integrated)**, principally via two
2026 programmes:

- **[Dana E-Sukan KBS (DEK)](/wiki/orgs/dek)** — grants for community tournaments
  and digital content.
- **[Malaysia E-sports Accelerator Program (MEAP)](/wiki/orgs/meap)** — support
  for athletes and teams competing internationally.

Together these were allocated **RM1.5 million** for 2026.
