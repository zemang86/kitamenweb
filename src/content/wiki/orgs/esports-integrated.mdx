## What is Esports Integrated?

**Esports Integrated (ESI)** is the **national esports initiative under
[KBS](/wiki/orgs/kbs)**, powered by **Impact Integrated**. It is the body that
turns government esports policy into delivery — running programmes, events and
grants to grow a sustainable Malaysian esports ecosystem.

## What ESI does

- **Administers [Dana E-Sukan KBS (DEK)](/wiki/orgs/dek)** — the KBS esports
  grant fund — and the **[MEAP](/wiki/orgs/meap)** accelerator.
- **Runs the Malaysia Esports League (MEL)** — a state-to-national grassroots
  pipeline operated with KBS and the
  **[Malaysia Esports Federation (MESF)](/wiki/orgs/mesf)**.

## Frequently asked questions

### Who runs Dana E-Sukan KBS?

Esports Integrated (ESI), the national esports initiative under the Ministry of
Youth and Sports.

### Is ESI a government body?

It is a national initiative under KBS, operated by Impact Integrated.
