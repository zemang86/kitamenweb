## What is MESF?

The **Malaysia Esports Federation (MESF)** is the **official national governing
body** for esports in Malaysia. The **Olympic Council of Malaysia (OCM)**
recognised MESF as the country's official national esports body in **November
2024**.

## Role and standing

MESF was first registered with the National Sports Commissioner in **2014** and
was promoted to **federation status in 2020**. It is an affiliate of the
**International Esports Federation (IESF)** and the **Asian Electronic Sports
Federation (AESF)**, and it coordinates Malaysia's **13 state esports
associations** — making it the custodian of national-team selection and
international representation.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is MESF the official esports body in Malaysia?

Yes — the Olympic Council of Malaysia recognised MESF as the official national
governing body for esports in November 2024.

### What is MESF affiliated with internationally?

The International Esports Federation (IESF) and the Asian Electronic Sports
Federation (AESF).
