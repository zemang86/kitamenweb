## What is Axle Sports?

**Axle Sports** (also Axle Motorsport) is a Malaysian **sim racing company and driver
academy** founded by former Formula 1 driver **[Alex Yoong](/wiki/players/alex-yoong)**,
based in **Petaling Jaya**. Through its **Axle Academy**, it coaches sim racers with courses
curated by Yoong, and it runs the **eRacing Grand Prix (eRacing GP)** competition series,
including Southeast Asia finals.

## A talent pipeline

Axle Sports is one of the two clearest anchors of Malaysian sim racing (alongside the
**[Toyota GR GT Cup Malaysia](/wiki/tournaments/toyota-gr-gt-cup-malaysia)**). It is where
Yoong discovered the **[Azlan brothers](/wiki/players/naquib-azlan)** — talent that went on
from the simulator to real-world racing — making the academy a genuine
**[sim-to-real](/wiki/glossary/sim-racing)** pipeline. Yoong's work here fed directly into
his appointment as the **[MESF](/wiki/orgs/mesf)** national e-Racing coach.

## Frequently asked questions

### Who founded Axle Sports?

Alex Yoong, Malaysia's only Formula 1 driver, who curates its academy courses.

### What does Axle Sports run?

A sim racing driver academy and the eRacing Grand Prix (eRacing GP) competition series, based
in Petaling Jaya.
