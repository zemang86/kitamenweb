## Eco round — definition

An **eco round** (short for **economy round**) is a round in
**[VALORANT](/wiki/games/valorant)** where a team **deliberately skips a full buy**
— saving credits instead of spending them on rifles, shields and abilities — so
they can afford a stronger purchase in a later round.

Managing the economy is a core part of tactical FPS strategy: teams weigh the cost
of "forcing" a buy now against saving to guarantee full equipment next round. The
same concept — and the term *eco* — applies in
**[Counter-Strike 2](/wiki/games/counter-strike-2)**, the game whose round-by-round
economy VALORANT borrowed from.
