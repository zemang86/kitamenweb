## Placement points — definition

**Placement points** are the part of a **[battle royale](/wiki/glossary/battle-royale)**
esports score that rewards **how high a team finishes** in a match. The higher you place,
the more points you earn — a **[WWCD](/wiki/glossary/wwcd)** (1st place) is worth the most.

In **[PUBG Mobile](/wiki/games/pubg-mobile)**, a team's total score blends **placement
points** with **kill (elimination) points** (typically +1 per elimination). That balance is
deliberate: pure aggression risks an early exit, while pure survival without kills caps your
ceiling — so the best teams manage both positioning and fights. (Exact point values vary by
event and season.)
