## Sentinel — definition

A **Sentinel** is one of the four agent roles in
**[VALORANT](/wiki/games/valorant)**. Sentinels are **defensive anchors**: they
**lock down sites and flanks**, hold positions with trap and slow abilities, and
gather intel so the team can react to enemy pushes.

Where **[Duelists](/wiki/glossary/duelist)** create space, Sentinels **deny** it —
watching a flank alone or holding a site while teammates rotate, which makes them
the backbone of a team's defence.
