## Balance of Performance — definition

**Balance of Performance (BoP)** is the set of **regulated adjustments** used to **equalise
different cars** so a varied field can race competitively. Organisers tweak parameters like
**weight (ballast), power, and aerodynamics** to bring fast and slow models closer together.

BoP is central to **GT3 and GT4** racing — and therefore to GT-focused sims like
**[Assetto Corsa Competizione](/wiki/games/assetto-corsa-competizione)** and
**[Gran Turismo](/wiki/games/gran-turismo)** — where dozens of real manufacturers' cars need to
be competitive against each other. Without BoP, one dominant model would win everything.
