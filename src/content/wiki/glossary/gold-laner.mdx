## Gold Laner — definition

The **gold laner** is the marksman role in
[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang). They are given
priority on the gold lane's resources — minions and the lane's gold-rich
objectives — so they can buy items quickly and become the team's primary
**late-game damage dealer**.

## What a gold laner does

- **Farms efficiently** to out-scale opponents into the late game.
- **Deals sustained damage** in team fights from a protected position.
- **Relies on the team** — the [roamer](/wiki/glossary/roamer) and
  [jungler](/wiki/glossary/jungler) shield the gold laner while they scale.

Because marksmen are fragile early but devastating late, the gold laner is a
high-priority target — and protecting them is a core job for the rest of the
team.

> **Bahasa Malaysia note:** Local casters typically say *"gold laner"* directly;
> the hero type is the *marksman* (MM).
