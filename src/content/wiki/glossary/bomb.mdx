## Bomb — definition

The **bomb** (the **C4**) is the central objective in
**[Counter-Strike 2](/wiki/games/counter-strike-2)**. The **attacking** team (Terrorists)
carries it and tries to **plant** it on one of the map's bomb sites; once planted, it counts
down to detonation. The **defending** team (Counter-Terrorists) wins by **defusing** it — or
by eliminating the attackers before the plant.

Because a planted bomb forces a **[retake](/wiki/glossary/retake)**, "getting the bomb down"
can win a round on the timer alone.

> In **[VALORANT](/wiki/games/valorant)**, the same objective device is called the
> **[Spike](/wiki/glossary/spike)** — VALORANT borrowed the plant/defuse structure directly
> from Counter-Strike.
