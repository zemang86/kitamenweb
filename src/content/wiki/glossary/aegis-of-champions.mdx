## Aegis of Champions — definition

The **Aegis of Champions** is the **physical trophy** — a heavy shield — awarded to the
winners of **[The International (TI)](/wiki/tournaments/the-international)**, the annual
**[Dota 2](/wiki/games/dota-2)** world championship. It is the most coveted prize in the
sport; champions are immortalised on it each year.

> Don't confuse it with the **Aegis of the Immortal**, the in-game item dropped by
> **[Roshan](/wiki/glossary/roshan)**. Same name, two very different things: one is a trophy
> you lift on stage, the other revives your hero mid-match.
