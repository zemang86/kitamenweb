## Roamer — definition

The **roamer** is the support role in
[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang). Usually a tank
or support hero, the roamer deliberately takes the least farm on the team so the
damage dealers can scale, and instead spends the game moving between lanes to
create advantages.

## What a roamer does

- **Protects the core** — peels for the [gold laner](/wiki/glossary/gold-laner)
  and mid laner in fights.
- **Sets up ganks** — roams to lanes with the [jungler](/wiki/glossary/jungler)
  to force kills.
- **Controls vision** — places and clears scouting around objectives.

A great roamer often shapes a series even without big damage numbers — which is
why a roamer like [Yums](/wiki/players/yums) can win finals MVP.

> **Bahasa Malaysia note:** In local commentary the role is usually called
> *"roamer"* directly; "support" and "tank" describe the hero types that play it.
