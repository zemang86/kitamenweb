## Mid Laner — definition

The **mid laner** holds the central lane in
[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang). The role is
almost always played by a **mage** — a hero with strong magic burst and
area-of-effect skills — making the mid laner a key source of damage and crowd
control from the middle of the map.

## What a mid laner does

- **Clears waves quickly** to free up time for roaming and rotations.
- **Bursts down** priority targets in team fights.
- **Links the map** — sitting in the centre, the mid laner can rotate to either
  side to back up the [gold laner](/wiki/glossary/gold-laner) or
  [EXP laner](/wiki/glossary/exp-laner).

Because the mid lane is short and central, the mid laner is expected to help the
[jungler](/wiki/glossary/jungler) contest objectives and to roam alongside the
[roamer](/wiki/glossary/roamer) once the lane is cleared.

> **Bahasa Malaysia note:** Local casters say *"mid laner"* directly; the hero
> type is the *mage*.
