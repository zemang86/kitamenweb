## Meta — definition

The **meta** (short for "most effective tactics available") is the set of
strategies, heroes and team compositions that are currently strongest in a game
like [Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang). It is not
fixed — it shifts every time the developer ships a balance [patch](/wiki/glossary/patch)
or when pro teams discover a new approach.

## Why the meta matters

- **Drafting** — the [pick & ban](/wiki/glossary/draft) phase revolves around
  which heroes are meta.
- **Climbing rank** — playing meta heroes is usually the fastest way up the
  [rank tiers](/wiki/glossary/rank-tiers).
- **Watching pro play** — the meta explains why certain heroes are picked or
  banned almost every game.

> **Bahasa Malaysia note:** Local players use *"meta"* directly — e.g. *"hero ni
> tengah meta"* (this hero is currently meta).
