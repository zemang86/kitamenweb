## Clutch — definition

A **clutch** is when the **last player alive** on a team **wins the round** despite
being outnumbered — the classic example being a **1v2 or 1v3** in
**[VALORANT](/wiki/games/valorant)**, where one player has to beat two or three
opponents alone.

Clutches often hinge on information and composure: tracking how many enemies remain,
using sound, and — on attack — sometimes winning purely by getting the
**[Spike](/wiki/glossary/spike)** down and playing the timer.
