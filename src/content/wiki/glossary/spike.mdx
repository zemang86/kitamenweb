## Spike — definition

The **Spike** is the **bomb** in **[VALORANT](/wiki/games/valorant)**. Each round,
the **attacking** team carries the Spike and tries to **plant** it on one of the
map's bomb sites; once planted, it counts down to detonation. The **defending**
team wins by **defusing** it (or eliminating the attackers before the plant).

Because a planted Spike forces defenders to retake the site, "getting the Spike
down" can win a round on the timer alone — central to attacking strategy and to
many **[clutch](/wiki/glossary/clutch)** situations.
