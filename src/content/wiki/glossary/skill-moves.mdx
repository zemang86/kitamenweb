## Skill moves — definition

**Skill moves** are the rated **dribbling tricks** in **[EA SPORTS FC](/wiki/games/ea-sports-fc)** —
flicks, rolls, feints and turns used to beat a defender. Each footballer has a **skill-move
rating from 1 to 5 stars**, and that rating **caps which moves they can perform**: only
5-star players can pull off the most advanced tricks.

A related stat, **weak foot** (also rated 1–5 stars), measures how accurately a player can
shoot and pass with their non-dominant foot. In high-level **1v1** play, skill moves are a
core tool for creating space and finishing chances.
