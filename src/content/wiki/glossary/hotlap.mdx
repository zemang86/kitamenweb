## Hotlap — definition

A **hotlap** is a **maximum-effort single lap** — pushing flat out for the fastest possible
time, with everything (the **[racing line](/wiki/glossary/racing-line)**, braking points,
throttle) executed perfectly. It contrasts with the more managed pace of a long race stint.

In **time trial** (or "hotlap") mode, a sim racer runs **alone against the clock**, chaining
hotlaps to lower their personal best. Many **[sim racing](/wiki/glossary/sim-racing)** ladders
— including Gran Turismo and iRacing — use time-trial times to seed or qualify drivers, making
the hotlap a core skill.
