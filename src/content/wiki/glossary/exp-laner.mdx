## EXP Laner — definition

The **EXP laner** plays the solo side lane in
[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang) — the lane
named for the **experience** the player soaks up there. The role is usually a
**fighter** or **tank**: a durable hero who can hold the lane alone and grow
into a frontline threat.

## What an EXP laner does

- **Wins the side lane** through sustained 1v1 pressure and split-pushing.
- **Builds a frontline** — soaks damage and initiates fights for the team.
- **Rotates to objectives** with the [jungler](/wiki/glossary/jungler) once the
  lane is stable.

Unlike the [gold laner](/wiki/glossary/gold-laner), the EXP laner prioritises
experience over gold, trading raw damage for durability and map presence.

> **Bahasa Malaysia note:** Local casters say *"EXP laner"* directly; the hero
> types are *fighter* and *tank*.
