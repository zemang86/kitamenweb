## Patch — definition

A **patch** is an update the developer releases for a game such as
[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang). A patch can
buff or nerf heroes, change items, adjust the map, or add new content. Patches
are identified by version numbers (for example, MLBB's Season 41 ran on patch
2.1.88).

## Why patches matter

- **They reshape the [meta](/wiki/glossary/meta)** — a buff can make a weak hero
  dominant overnight.
- **Pros read patch notes** — the official notes that ship with each patch tell
  competitive players what changed and why.
- **They reset rank strategy** — what worked last patch in the
  [rank tiers](/wiki/glossary/rank-tiers) may not work in the next.

> **Bahasa Malaysia note:** Local players say *"patch baru"* (new patch) and
> *"patch notes"* directly.
