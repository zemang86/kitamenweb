## Controller — definition

A **Controller** is one of the four agent roles in
**[VALORANT](/wiki/games/valorant)**. Controllers **shape the battlefield** with
**smokes and area-denial** abilities — cutting off sightlines and slicing the map
into pieces the team can take one fight at a time.

Good smoke placement lets a team **execute onto a site** (attack) or **retake**
it (defence) on favourable terms, which is why Controllers work hand-in-hand with
**[Initiators](/wiki/glossary/initiator)** and **[Duelists](/wiki/glossary/duelist)**.
