## Initiator — definition

An **Initiator** is one of the four agent roles in
**[VALORANT](/wiki/games/valorant)**. Initiators **set up takes**: they use
intel, flashes and disruption abilities to **clear angles and blind or displace
defenders**, creating the safe opening their
**[Duelist](/wiki/glossary/duelist)** entries through.

Initiators pair tightly with Controllers and Duelists on a site execute, and with
**[Sentinels](/wiki/glossary/sentinel)** when gathering information on defence.
