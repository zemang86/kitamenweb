## Gloo Wall — definition

The **Gloo Wall** is a deployable item in **[Garena Free Fire](/wiki/games/free-fire)**
that instantly creates a **makeshift wall** wherever you throw it, giving cover in
the open. It is one of Free Fire's defining mechanics — there is no equivalent in
most other battle royales.

## Why the Gloo Wall matters

- **Instant cover** in open ground, where no natural cover exists.
- **Peek-and-shoot** play: skilled players break and re-place walls to control
  angles in a fight.
- **Rotations and revives**: walls buy time to heal, revive a teammate, or move
  ahead of the shrinking [safe zone](/wiki/glossary/battle-royale).

Gloo Wall control is a core competitive skill in Malaysian
[FFMC](/wiki/tournaments/free-fire-malaysia-championship) play.
