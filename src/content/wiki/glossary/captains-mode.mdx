## Captain's Mode — definition

**Captain's Mode (CM)** is the **standard competitive draft format** in
**[Dota 2](/wiki/games/dota-2)**. Before the match, each team's **captain** alternately
**bans** heroes (removing them for both sides) and **picks** the five heroes their team will
play, following a fixed sequence.

Drafting well is a discipline in itself: captains target counters, deny power picks, and
build a line-up that fits their **[five positions](/wiki/glossary/position-system)** — so a
game can be shaped before a single creep spawns.
