## Entry fragger — definition

An **entry fragger** is the player who **leads the team's push onto a site**,
deliberately **taking the first duel** to create space and information for the rest
of the team. The job is high-risk: the entry often trades their life to open a site,
but a won opening duel swings the round.

In **[VALORANT](/wiki/games/valorant)**, the entry fragger almost always plays a
**[Duelist](/wiki/glossary/duelist)**, whose aggressive abilities are built exactly
for that first contact. It is a function, not an official agent role — the
counterpart to the team's **[in-game leader](/wiki/glossary/igl)**, who decides
*where* and *when* to make that entry.
