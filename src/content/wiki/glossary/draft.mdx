## Draft — definition

The **draft** — also called the **pick & ban** — is the phase before a
competitive [Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang)
match where the two teams take turns **banning** heroes (removing them from the
game) and **picking** the heroes they will play. It produces each team's
**composition** for that game.

## Why the draft matters

- **It is half the game** — a strong draft can win a match before it starts.
- **It is shaped by the [meta](/wiki/glossary/meta)** — the most powerful heroes
  are usually banned first.
- **Roles drive picks** — teams draft to fill each role, from the
  [gold laner](/wiki/glossary/gold-laner) to the [roamer](/wiki/glossary/roamer),
  while countering the opponent.

> **Bahasa Malaysia note:** Local casters say *"draft"*, *"ban"* and *"pick"*
> directly during the picks-and-bans phase.
