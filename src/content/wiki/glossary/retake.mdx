## Retake — definition

A **retake** is when the **defending** team moves **back onto a bomb site after the bomb has
been planted**, trying to clear the attackers and **defuse** before it detonates. It is the
opposite of holding a site: instead of defending from the start, the team has lost initial
control and must win it back under a ticking clock.

Retakes demand coordination — using utility to clear angles and trading kills efficiently —
and often come down to a **[clutch](/wiki/glossary/clutch)**. The concept applies in
**[Counter-Strike 2](/wiki/games/counter-strike-2)** and, identically, in
**[VALORANT](/wiki/games/valorant)** after a **[Spike](/wiki/glossary/spike)** plant.
