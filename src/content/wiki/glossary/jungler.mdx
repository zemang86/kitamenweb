## Jungler — definition

The **jungler** is a role in MOBA games such as
[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang). Instead of
holding a lane, the jungler clears **neutral monster camps** (the "jungle")
between lanes to gain gold and experience faster, then uses that tempo to
**gank** — surprise-attack enemies in lane — and to contest map objectives.

## What a jungler does

- **Farms** neutral camps efficiently to out-level opponents early.
- **Ganks** lanes to create kills and pressure.
- **Secures objectives** like the lord/turtle that swing the game.

A strong jungler dictates the early-game tempo, which is why the role is often
trusted to a team's most mechanically skilled player.
