## AWP — definition

The **AWP** is the iconic **sniper rifle** in **[Counter-Strike 2](/wiki/games/counter-strike-2)** —
a **one-shot kill** to the body that can hold an angle or win a round on its own. It is
expensive, so buying it is an economic commitment, and a missed shot leaves you vulnerable
at close range.

Most teams have a dedicated **AWPer** whose job is to anchor key positions and trade picks.
Malaysia's best-known CS export, **[kaze](/wiki/players/kaze)**, made his name as an AWPer on
the international circuit.
