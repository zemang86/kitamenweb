## Rank tiers — definition

**Rank tiers** are the divisions of the competitive ladder in ranked matchmaking.
In [Mobile Legends: Bang Bang](/wiki/games/mobile-legends-bang-bang) the ladder
rises through **Warrior, Elite, Master, Grandmaster, Epic, Legend** and then
**Mythic** — the top tier, which is itself split into sub-grades (Mythic, Mythical
Honor, Mythical Glory, and the Mythical Immortal leaderboard).

## Why rank tiers matter

- **Mythic is the proving ground** — aspiring pros grind the top of the Mythic
  ladder to get noticed.
- **The [meta](/wiki/glossary/meta) changes by tier** — higher tiers punish
  off-meta picks far more.
- **Each season resets** — a new [patch](/wiki/glossary/patch)/season usually
  drops your rank, so the climb restarts.

> **Bahasa Malaysia note:** Local players say *"push rank"* (climb the ladder)
> and *"Mythic"* directly.
