## Duelist — definition

A **Duelist** is one of the four agent roles in
**[VALORANT](/wiki/games/valorant)**. Duelists are **self-sufficient, aggressive**
agents whose abilities are built around **taking fights and creating space** —
they lead the charge onto a site and look to win the opening duel.

Because of that aggressive job, the team's **[entry fragger](/wiki/glossary/entry-fragger)**
is almost always a Duelist. The other three roles are
**[Initiator](/wiki/glossary/initiator)**,
**[Controller](/wiki/glossary/controller)** and
**[Sentinel](/wiki/glossary/sentinel)**.
