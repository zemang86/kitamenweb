## Roshan — definition

**Roshan** is **[Dota 2's](/wiki/games/dota-2)** most powerful **neutral boss** — a creep
that lives in its own pit and grows stronger over the game. Killing it drops the **Aegis of
the Immortal**, an item that **revives its carrier once** shortly after death, plus other
bounties.

Because the Aegis can swing a teamfight or secure a push, **"Roshan control"** — timing the
kill and contesting the pit — is one of the core strategic objectives in competitive Dota 2.

> Don't confuse the **Aegis of the Immortal** (the in-game item from Roshan) with the
> **[Aegis of Champions](/wiki/glossary/aegis-of-champions)** — the physical trophy awarded
> to the winners of The International.
