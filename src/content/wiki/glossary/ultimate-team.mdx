## Ultimate Team — definition

**Ultimate Team (FUT)** is **[EA SPORTS FC's](/wiki/games/ea-sports-fc)** flagship mode,
where players **collect footballer cards** and build a custom squad to compete with. It is
the most popular mode in the game — and, crucially, the **basis of the competitive format**:
pro EA FC is played **1v1 in Ultimate Team** on EA's **FC Pro** circuit.

This is distinct from **Pro Clubs**, the co-op 11v11 mode where each player controls a single
footballer — popular, but **not** the format used in the pro circuit.
