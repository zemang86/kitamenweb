## Battle Royale — definition

A **battle royale (BR)** is a game mode in which a large number of players —
solo or in squads — are dropped onto a map with minimal gear and fight to be the
**last one standing**. A **safe zone** steadily shrinks over the match, forcing
players closer together until a winner emerges.

In **[Garena Free Fire](/wiki/games/free-fire)**, a BR match drops up to **50
players**; squads of up to four scavenge weapons, **rotate** ahead of the zone,
and try to survive the final circles. It is the mode behind Free Fire, PUBG
Mobile and similar mobile titles.

## What defines a battle royale

- **Many players, one winner** — last player or squad standing wins.
- **Shrinking play area** — a closing safe zone forces engagements.
- **Scavenge-and-survive** — players start with nothing and loot the map.
