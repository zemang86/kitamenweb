## Booyah — definition

**"Booyah!"** is the celebratory message **[Garena Free Fire](/wiki/games/free-fire)**
displays when your squad **wins the match** as the last one standing. It is Free
Fire's signature victory screen — the game's take on the battle-royale "Winner
Winner" moment.

The word has become shorthand for a Free Fire win in general: players talk about
"getting a Booyah," and Garena has used it across community campaigns and the
in-app *Booyah!* branding.
