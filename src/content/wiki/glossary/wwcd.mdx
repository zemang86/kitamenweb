## WWCD — definition

**WWCD** stands for **"Winner Winner Chicken Dinner"** — the phrase
**[PUBG Mobile](/wiki/games/pubg-mobile)** displays when you win a match by being the
**last player or squad standing**. The line dates back to the original PUBG, and in esports
it has become the standard shorthand for a **match win**.

In tournament play, a WWCD is also worth the maximum **[placement points](/wiki/glossary/placement-points)**,
so "getting the WWCD" means both winning the round and topping its placement score. The
equivalent term in **[Garena Free Fire](/wiki/games/free-fire)** is a
**[Booyah](/wiki/glossary/booyah)**.
